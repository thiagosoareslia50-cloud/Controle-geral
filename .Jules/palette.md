## 2024-05-18 - [Dynamic ARIA labels for toggle buttons]
**Learning:** Toggle buttons (like filtering by all or by specific) can benefit from dynamic `aria-label`s and `title`s based on their current state, to accurately convey what action the button will perform if clicked.
**Action:** When adding accessibility attributes to buttons that toggle between states, ensure the labels are dynamic and reflect the *next* state or action.
