## 2024-05-09 - Dynamic ARIA labels for icon-only toggles
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons with toggling states must have dynamic `aria-label`s reflecting the specific action or state context, rather than just describing the current icon.
**Action:** When adding accessibility features to toggle buttons, ensure the label text dynamically reflects the action (e.g., 'Ver todos' vs 'Filtrar por histórico') matching any tooltip.
